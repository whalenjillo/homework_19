ABOUT ME UI/UX GRAPHIC DESIGN CONTACT

Jill is a Graphic Designer & a UI/UX Designer
living and working in Houston, TX

Jill is a bilingual (fluent in Spanish) UI/UX Designer with a background in Graphic Design. She's passionate about creating beautiful and user-friendly products that provide an enhanced experience for users. Jill has worked with incredibly talented people across multiple corporate companies, non-profits, and small businesses.

After being a Graphic Designer for 6 years, Jill developed an interest in User Interface/User Experience Design as it became more popular. As a designer, she wanted to expand her expertise in the realm of design and once she dove in to UI/UX, she knew she wanted to move her career in this direction. It's a joy and a creative journey for her to combine graphic design while integrating the user experience of a product.
